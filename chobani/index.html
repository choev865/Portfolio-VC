CHOBANI ADVERTISEMENT

This project is a visually captivating animation adverstisement for a new madeup Chobani yogurt flavor, Honey Chocolate Dream. This visually captivating advertisement unfolds with a symphony of fun transitions, skillfully introducing the delectable new ingredients that bring the flavor to life.

Through digital drawings, this animation transcends the ordinary, breathing dynamic energy into each frame. The motion sequences and thoughtfully integrated sound elements elevate the animation, turning it into a multisensory piece that complements the sensory experience of the envisioned yogurt flavor. The advertisement aims to immerse the audience in the enticing world of Honey Chocolate Dream, inviting them to savor the flavor even before it exists.
