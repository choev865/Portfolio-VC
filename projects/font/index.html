2ECOND CHANCE
2ECOND CHANCE is a website for people who are looking for inspiration to revive their old goods. By taking trash on the streets or items you would normally throw away, the website recontextualizes it to create a new image of trash to treasure. The shopping element and layout is ironic to the whole message because you are trying to buy something you were meaning to throw out, which is why you cannot actually buy anything. The website touches on environmental impacts and how upcycling these "useless" items gives it a new life and meaning.
