BREAK FROM REALITY

This rotoscope animation depicts a glimpse in my life, highlighting the ordinary and the unordinary. The project began as a true-to-life depiction of my daily routine, employing rotoscope animation to capture the nuances of movement and emotion.

This animation goes beyond the mundane, subtly weaving in aspirations to break free from the routine, creating a layer of relatable depth. The careful attention to emotions, expressions, and the fluidity of motion breathes life into each frame, transforming the project into a dynamic exploration of the human experience.

More than a personal narrative, this project serves as a testament to the universal need for whimsy and spontaneity in our lives. It encourages viewers to discern the hidden wishes within their own routines, urging them to appreciate the beauty of imagination and playfulness amid the backdrop of the ordinary. Through this animation, I aim to inspire others to find joy in the seemingly mundane, fostering a deeper connection with the magic that lies within the routine of our everyday lives.
