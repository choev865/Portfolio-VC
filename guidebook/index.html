GUIDE TO YOUR 20'S

"Guide to Your 20s" is a dynamic and innovative approach to navigating the transformative decade of one's 20s. Departing from the traditional format, this accordion-shaped book challenges expectations and embraces a unique design that mirrors the diverse and unfolding journey to get to your twenties.

The book unfolds like a visual journey, with each crease of the accordion revealing a folded sheet of paper that can be flipped or spread out, adding an interactive dimension to the reading experience. Through this accordion design, I've incorporated advice spanning various aspects of life, neatly categorized into sections on culture, independence, and mental health, each accompanied by thought-provoking manifestos.

Beyond the content, the layout of the full pages forms intricate images that symbolize the progression from a younger self to an older self as well as reflecting the transformative paths that lead to the pivotal age of 20. This visual representation serves as a powerful metaphor for the evolving journey, capturing the essence of growth and self-discovery.

"Guide to Your 20s" is an artistic exploration of life's complexities during this transformative decade. Through its innovative design, compelling advice, and visual storytelling, this guide invites readers to unfold and discover the wealth of knowledge within its pages, making the journey through the 20s both informative and visually captivating.
